Our Mission

Compassionate care, made possible.

"

NeuroHealth Foundation provides neurofeedback services, scholarships, and funding support to underserved populations in the Treasure Valley of Boise, Idaho — opening doors to healing that has too often been out of reach.

What We Do

Three ways we serve.

Neurofeedback Sessions

Direct, sponsored access to clinical neurofeedback for children, veterans, and families navigating financial hardship.

Scholarships

Need-based scholarships that cover full or partial treatment costs — reviewed quickly, awarded with dignity.

Community Funding

Grants and partnerships that strengthen local mental-health providers and expand neurofeedback in the valley.

Clinical Partner

Services delivered by Boise Neurofeedback.

We fund the care. Boise Neurofeedback provides it — with experienced clinicians, modern equipment, and personalized protocols for every client we sponsor.
